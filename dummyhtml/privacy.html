Privacy Policy

At our Bakery, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, phone number, and delivery address when placing an order.
  • Payment details for processing transactions securely.
  • Browsing information such as cookies and usage data to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and deliver your bakery orders.
  • Communicate order updates and promotional offers (if you opt-in).
  • Improve our website, services, and customer support.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no online transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute safety.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may only be shared with trusted service providers (such as payment processors or delivery partners) to complete your order.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please check this page regularly for the latest version.
